About Wheatbelt Ag Precision

Wheatbelt Ag Precision is focused on bringing practical, localised Precision Ag services to growers across the central Wheatbelt. We combine on-ground experience with modern tools to help you turn variability into better decisions and more confident input use.

Local, practical and grower-focused

Being based in Merredin means we understand the realities of farming in this region—soil types, seasons, machinery and logistics. We’re not here to drop a set of maps and disappear; we work with you to make sure everything fits your system.

Whether it’s a single paddock that never quite performs, or a full-farm strategy you want to build over several seasons, we can help map it out and support the setup.

What we care about

  • Clear, honest communication with growers and agronomists.
  • Maps and systems that work with the gear you already own.
  • Simple reporting that makes sense at the kitchen table.
  • Building long-term value into your paddocks and data.
